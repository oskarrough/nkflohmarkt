Performance von UMLAUT! um 16 Uhr


UMLAUT! are Cosmo Wise and Sam Bardsley, a British duo based in Berlin. They have released their single "U!" on Valley Sound Recordings and have a new LP release expected shortly! Their electronic pop has catchy melodies and plenty of humour within.

Sounds a bit like a bastard child of Brian Eno and Devo.
