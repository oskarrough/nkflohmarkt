This is why we're so excited to have finally found a location that is large enough to host the same flea market you know and love, INDOORS. CANK, the iconic, brutalistic and now abandoned department store is a temporary location in the heart of Berlin-Neukölln.

Duration of market: 12:00 - 19:00h
Entrance fee for visitors: 2,50€ per person

Our stands

Our stands cost 40€ each. Each seller is allowed to purchase up to a maximum 4 stands for each market. If you purchase more than one stand, we will ensure that these stands are placed directly next to each other. The stands are sold on a "first come, first served" basis. We don't take reservations and there will be no extra stands added once we are sold out.

Each seller has a 3m x 2m (6m2) space to sell within. You will automatically be provided with a 3m x 0,88m table, however if you do not require one, please inform us beforehand and we can remove it for you. The stands do not have a roof and we do not provide seating.

Why is there an entrance fee for visitors?

As opposed to our regular market at Kranoldplatz, there are many additional costs that are involved to make these indoor events possible. These include things such as rental costs, heating, lighting, security, staff etc. We have tried to keep the entrance fee as low as possible to cover these costs and hope that you understand our reasoning for charging.

Against discrimination

We are against any form of discrimination and violence. Sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, ableistic or similar attacks will not be tolerated.

Booking & Registration

EVENT LOCATION:

BOOKING A STALL

Stands can only be paid for with PayPal, however you do not require a PayPal account to purchase one. You are also able to pay as a "guest" and will then be able to use your EC or Credit Card.

All stand sales are final and cannot be rescheduled or refunded.

REGISTRATION AND SET UP

  • If you are arriving by car, please unload your belongings in our designated area and immediately move your vehicle, so that others may also do the same.
  • Please register at our Infostand between 10:00 - 10:30. Your name will be registered under the name of your PayPal account. You will then be given your stand number and given directions where to find it. Each registered seller is allowed to bring one extra guest free of charge into the event. Every extra guest will need to pay the entrance fee.
  • It is not possible to request where you will be placed on the day of the market. If you have a particular request (ie. you have mobility issues and need to sell on the ground floor, you want to be placed next to a friend etc) you must email info@nkflohmarkt.de on the Monday before the market at the very latest. There is still no guarantee that we are able to fulfil this request, however we will try our best.
  • Please have your stand set up and ready to sell by 12:00 when the event officially begins.
  • It is not permitted to use nails, screws, staples and anything else which could damage the stalls. You will be held responsible for any damage caused to the stall.
  • It is forbidden to sell any food or drinks at your stand.
  • LEAVE NOTHING BEHIND! Out of respect for our team who have to clean the space, it is forbidden to leave any of your belongings behind.
  • Beware of thieves! Do not leave valuable items unattended.
